GCSE English Language Rubric - Paper 2

AO1: Understanding ideas and perspectives (10 points)
Identify and interpret explicit and implicit information and ideas

AO2: Analysis of language and structure (15 points)
Explain, comment on and analyse how writers use language and structure to achieve effects

AO3: Comparison of ideas and perspectives (10 points)
Compare writers' ideas and perspectives, as well as how these are conveyed

AO4: Technical accuracy in writing (15 points)
Evaluate texts critically and support this with appropriate textual references

Grammar and Spelling (10 points)
Demonstrate clear and accurate written expression
